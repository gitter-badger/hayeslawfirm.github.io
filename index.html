Principals

Ben Hayes, Esq.

Benjamin Hayes represents individual and corporate clients in establishing and growing technology start-ups. He has advised on areas including incorporation, business operation, intellectual property, financing, international tax, labor, and immigration law. Prior to forming Hayes Law Firm, PLLC, he worked with law firms in Houston, Washington, D.C., New York, Tokyo, and Osaka in fields including international intellectual property licensing and contracts, insurance litigation, bankruptcy, and large-scale products liability and securities litigation. Ben is admitted to practice law in New York and is a member of the New York State Bar Association, the New York County Lawyers’ Association, the Japanese American Lawyers Network, and the Roppongi Bar Assocation.
